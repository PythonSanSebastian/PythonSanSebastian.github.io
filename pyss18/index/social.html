Pyntxos Night

When:Friday Night, 19.00 to ~24.00.

Where:Old part of San Sebastian.

Price:Around 25€ (pay only for what you eat).

Pintxo is a small snack, typically eaten in bars, traditional in northern Spain and especially popular in the Basque country and Navarre. They are usually eaten in bars or taverns as a small snack while hanging out with friends or relatives; thus, they have a strong socializing component, and in the Basque country and Navarre they are usually regarded as a cornerstone of local culture and society
